Short Bio

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where I have the privilege of being advised by Prof. Wei Wang and collaborating closely with Prof. Yizhou Sun. My research interests focus on post-training for Large Language Models and Machine Reasoning. I earned my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) in May 2022.
